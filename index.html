Frequently Asked Questions

Is my journal data private?

Absolutely. All your entries are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your personal reflections.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Lullen+ subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Lullen, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

What happens to my data if I cancel Lullen+?

Your data stays on your device. You'll still have access to the last 30 days in the free version. Consider exporting your data before canceling if you want to keep everything.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Lullen is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your personal reflections should remain personal.

Data Collection

Lullen stores all journal entries, mood data, and personal content locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your journal content.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your journal entries or mood data
  • Personal identifying information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally and does not include any journal content.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for information about how they handle payment data.

Data Export

Lullen+ subscribers can export their data in JSON or PDF format. This data export is generated locally on your device.

Contact

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
